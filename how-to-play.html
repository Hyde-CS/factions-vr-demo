Starting Out

  • Download the latest build from GitHub Releases
  • Launch in VR (supports teleportation & free movement)
  • Use voice chat to interact with other players
  • Explore, build, trade, or join community events

Tips & Tricks

  • Always follow the laws to avoid penalties
  • Coordinate with other players for city-building
  • Keep track of court rulings if you plan to participate in governance
