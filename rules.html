Foundational Laws

  • No griefing or stealing at spawn
  • No exploiting glitches or dupes
  • No hate speech or targeting

Build Permissions

  • Do not place random blocks
  • Notify judges if building in contested zones
  • Do not abuse permissions for unfair advantage

Crime Severity

Petty Crimes (1-2 days)

  • Minor theft
  • Random killing (low degree)

Misdemeanors (2-5 days)

  • Exploits or fraud in trades
  • Misuse of build permissions

Felonies (7-14 days)

  • Robbing spawn buildings
  • Destruction/griefing without permission
