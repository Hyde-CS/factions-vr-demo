Welcome to Factions VR

Factions VR is a multiplayer voxel sandbox experience in virtual reality. Explore, build, and interact in a constantly evolving world where creativity and community shape the environment.

Join players worldwide, build your own cities, follow laws, participate in courts, and witness the world evolve. Factions VR combines active locomotion, voice chat, and persistent builds to create a dynamic social VR experience.

Rules & Laws

Foundational Laws

  • Properly use the buildings as intended
  • No robbing of any property (in spawn)
  • No glitching through walls/floors
  • No targeting
  • No stealing
  • No duping for personal gain (SERVER-WIDE)
  • No hate speech (SERVER-WIDE)
  • Using trickery or fraud in trades (SERVER-WIDE)

Extra Rules for People with Build Perms

  • Don't place random blocks
  • Let judges know if your building somewhere
  • Don't use perms to your advantage (trapping people, breaking sealed buildings, etc.)
  • No random killing
  • No destruction of any building without permission

Crime Rankings

Petty Crimes: 1-2 days

  • Stealing (depends on degree)
  • Hate speech
  • Targeting
  • Random killing (depends on degree)
  • Using buildings how they're not supposed to be used
  • Don't place random blocks

Misdemeanors: 2-5 days

  • Glitching through walls/floors for malicious intent
  • Using trickery or fraud in trades
  • Using build perms for advantage

Felonies: 7-14 days

  • Robbing any building in spawn
  • Destroying/griefing spawn buildings without permission
  • No duping
